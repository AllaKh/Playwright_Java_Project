Issue 2: Amenities link adds hashtag but does not navigate

Summary:
Clicking the Amenities link appends a hashtag (#amenities) to the URL but does not scroll or navigate to the Amenities section.

Description:
On the homepage, clicking the Amenities link modifies the URL by adding #amenities as a fragment identifier (e.g., https://automationintesting.online/#amenities), but the page does not scroll or jump to the Amenities section as expected. This behavior causes confusion for users who expect to be navigated to that section.

Steps to Reproduce:

  1. Open https://automationintesting.online/
  2. Click on the "Amenities" link in the navigation or menu.
  3. Observe that the URL changes to include #amenities but the page position does not change.

Actual Result:
URL updates with #amenities but the page remains at the top; no scrolling or navigation occurs.

Expected Result:
Clicking the Amenities link should smoothly scroll or jump the viewport to the Amenities section of the page.

Severity: Low
